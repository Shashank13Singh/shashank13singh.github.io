About Us

Lalta Prasad Singh Institute of Technology, part of Kiran Siksha Seva Samiti, started with an Industrial Training Institute in the year 2012 and now adding a polytechnic college to its basket. where, extraordinary faculty and students seek answers to complex questions, discover new knowledge, lead innovation, and transform our future. LPSIT is a place of extraordinary interactions. It's a setting where people and ideas come together in new ways—illuminating mysteries, making sparks fly, and fostering intellectual breakthroughs.

The mission of our institute is to expand human knowledge and benefit society. We educate our students to become creative members of society. The Institute is committed to generating, disseminating, and preserving knowledge, and to working with others to bring this knowledge to bear on the world's great challenges. LPSIT is dedicated to providing its students with an education that combines rigorous academic study and the excitement of discovery with the support and intellectual stimulation of a diverse campus community. We seek to develop in each member of the LPSIT community the ability and passion to work wisely, creatively, and effectively for the betterment of humankind.

Departments

The institute is privately endowed, coeducational, organised into five branches.

  • Mechanical

    Mechanical department is one of the biggest departments of the institute. While managing this increased strength which is an urgent need of the nation, department is committed to well being and all round development of its students.

  • Electrical

    This department has distinguised faculty. The infrastructure and lab facilities are upgraded from time to time and provide adequate oppertunities for students to learn and inovate.

  • Electronics

    The Department of Electronics Engineering has highly qualified and esteemed faculty members. In tune with the latest developments and keeping pace with the rapidly evolving technology, specialized laboratories have been established with a view to strengthen research and development.

  • Civil

    This is one of the primary engineering department of the institute. The department actively promotes curriculum development activity. The Department contributes to the interdisciplinary academic and research activity of the Institute.
